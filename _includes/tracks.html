Big Blue Bus

Learn more about bus scheduling using the GTFS and GTFS-rt data standards.

The General Transit Feed Specification (GTFS) is a common standard used by transit agencies to publish transportation schedules. The Big Blue Bus has made static timetables available via GTFS for over a year.

We are excited to announce the public release of GTFS-rt for displaying real-time information.

Come learn more about our GTFS data and how you may use it for your own applications. This track will be a deep dive into transit-oriented data, allowing you to interact and ask questions to those most familiar with the data.

Parking

Showcase your skills by building a sample app using real-time or historical parking data.

Real-time parking information, both for on-street meters and public lots and structures, has consistently been one of our most highly requested datasets. This data has historically been locked behind proprietary systems and Non-Disclosure Agreements.

We are excited to announce the public launch of a new Parking Data API (and associated documentation!)

However, we don't yet have any samples showcasing the new API. This session will be hands-on; bring your laptop and get ready to learn how to use the API, then get creative and build lightweight apps which explore and/or visualize the data.
