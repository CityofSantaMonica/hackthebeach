Your application submission should be an 8 slide deck, addressing the sections below. See our sample template for an example.

Slide 1: Cover

Your Project's Name and your Team's Name

Slide 2: Team Overview

List your team members

  • Names
  • Roles
  • Responsibilities

Summarize your qualifications

  • Education
  • Professional Experience

Identify a (Single) Primary Contact

  • Name
  • Email

Slide 3: Project Overview

  • Description
  • Intended users/demographic
  • Benefits
  • Strategic initiative served (community, mobility, civic engagement)
    • Specify which initiative(s) is/are served, and how.

Slide 4: Technology Elements

  • How is technology incorporated in your product?
  • What technologies will be used by you?
  • What technologies will be used by consumers of your product?

Note that technology doesnâ€™t have to be at the core of your product. For example, you may create a marketing plan which utilizes technology in the form of social media for pushing messages.

Slide 5: Visual Elements

  • Branding
  • Wireframe
  • Prototype

Slide 6: Value

List the value propositions for your product. Who will use it, and why?

Slide 7: Viability

How long will it take to build your product?

  • List milestones along with dates

How much will it cost to build your product?

  • List funding sources

Slide 8: Growth

How will your product grow over time?
